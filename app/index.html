Carolina Personnel Services is a locally owned and operated corporation. Established in 1986, Carolina Personnel has been serving the Midlands temporary, temporary to hire, Direct Hire, Contract and permanent staffing needs successfully. We offer a variety of employment opportunities on a permanent or temporary basis. We have updated software available in our office to help you increase your skills in all Microsoft applications. Carolina Personnel Services never charges a fee to our potential employees. Carolina Personnel is a drug and alcohol free workplace.
