Carolina Personnel Services is a locally owned and operated corporation tht has been serving the staffing needs of the Midlands since 1986.

We offer a variety of permanent and temporary employment opportunities to our jobseekers. Additionally, we firmly believe in doing our best to help you reach the next stage in your career, and therefore provide opportunities in our office to help you increase your skills in all Microsoft applications.

Carolina Personnel Services never charges a fee to our potential employees.

Carolina Personnel is a drug and alcohol free workplace.

Minimum Requirements & Qualifications:
  • Must be over the age of 18.
  • Must have a Minimum of 1 year working experience, in the area that you are applying.
  • Must have a Valid, Non-Expired, Drivers License or ID. Valid Passport also accepted.
  • Must have a Birth Certificate or Social Security Card. Valid Passport also accepted.
  • Must have Reliable Transportation.
  • Must have a Current Resume, to include most recent employment, showing dates of employment from Month/Year - Month/Year.
  • Must have and be able to provide a copy of HS Diploma or GED. High School or College Transcripts also accepted.
  • Must be able to pass a Background Check and Drug Screen.
